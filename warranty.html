Our online store cooperates only with those manufacturers who carefully monitor the quality of their products. But in rare cases, unfortunately, it also happens that the goods purchased in the store are nevertheless manufactured with defects or damaged during transportation. In such cases, a guarantee comes to the help of the buyer, which is provided for the entire range of our store. On this page, we will tell you what you need to do to protect yourself as much as possible from such cases.

Upon receipt of the goods

Regardless of the delivery method you choose, there are several mandatory steps that must be performed upon receipt of the goods before signing the delivery acceptance on the waybill:

  1. To be present at the acceptance of the goods in person or to transfer such a right to your representative
  2. Inspect the appearance and integrity of the original packaging
  3. Inspect all glass elements (including mirrors) for integrity (chips, cracks, scratches, etc.)
  4. Inspect all facades for various defects (scratches, chips, etc.)
  5. Check the completeness of the goods, color, content, quantity, etc. according to the order form. Replacement of the above parts after the acceptance of the goods (furniture) is at the expense of the customer.
  6. Save all documents provided to you by our company.

Only after you have made sure of the proper quality and condition of the delivered products, sign the delivery acceptance documents. Otherwise, contact us at one of the specified phone numbers, and our warranty department will help you solve the situation.

Out-of-warranty service

Our online store values ​​every customer. Therefore, even if the goods you purchased are damaged on their own, or after the expiration of the warranty period, the store employees are ready to come to your aid. Out-of-warranty and post-warranty service means that the seller, at the buyer's request, can arrange for the repair of defective products or their parts. In this case, the costs of delivery of the complaint product, in both directions, and the cost of the work on the complaint itself are paid by the buyer.
